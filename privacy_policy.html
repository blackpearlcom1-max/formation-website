01

Who We Are

Formation Bible Study is a Bible study application operated by Berean Defense LLC, a company based in the United States. When this policy says "Formation Bible Study," "we," "us," or "our," it refers to Berean Defense LLC and the Formation Bible Study application at formationbible.com.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle personal information collected through the Formation Bible Study app, website, and related services. By using Formation Bible Study, you agree to the practices described here.

02

What We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly, and information collected automatically when you use the app.

Information You Provide

person

Account Information

Your name and email address when you join the waitlist or create an account. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those providers.

credit_card

Payment Information

Billing information for paid plans. We do not store your full card number — payments are processed by Stripe, which handles all sensitive billing data on our behalf.

menu_book

Study Content

Notes, highlights, bookmarks, and study plans you create within the app. This content is yours — we store it to provide the service and never use it for advertising.

chat

Communications

If you contact us by email, we retain your message to respond and improve our service. We won't share it with third parties except as necessary to answer your question.

Information Collected Automatically

Data Type Examples Purpose
Device & Browser OS, browser type, screen size, language Bug fixes and compatibility
Usage Data Pages visited, features used, session duration Product improvement and analytics
Log Data IP address, timestamps, error reports Security monitoring and debugging
Cookies Session tokens, preference flags, analytics IDs Authentication and analytics

See our Cookie Policy for full details on cookies.

05

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (such as billing records, which may be kept for up to 7 years for tax purposes).

Data Retention Period
Account & profile data Until account deletion + 30 days
Study notes, highlights, bookmarks Until account deletion + 30 days
Billing records 7 years (tax & legal compliance)
Server & access logs 90 days (security)
Anonymized analytics Indefinite (no personal identifiers)
06

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

https

TLS Encryption

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.

storage

Encrypted at Rest

Your data is stored in encrypted databases managed by Supabase.

key

Secure Authentication

Passwords are hashed. OAuth login options (Google, Apple) never expose your password to us.

manage_accounts

Limited Access

Only authorized team members can access user data, and only when necessary.

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to privacy@formation.app.

Your Rights & Choices

You have meaningful control over your information. Regardless of where you live, we honor these rights for all Formation Bible Study users.

visibility

Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

edit

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information in your account settings or by contacting us.

delete

Deletion

Delete your account and associated personal data. We'll process this within 30 days.

download

Portability

Export your study notes, highlights, and bookmarks in a standard format.

unsubscribe

Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the link in any email we send.

cookie

08

Children's Privacy

Formation Bible Study is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@formation.app and we will delete that information promptly.

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 may use Formation Bible Study with verifiable parental or guardian consent.

09

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If changes are material, we'll notify you by email or with a prominent notice in the app at least 14 days before the changes take effect.

Your continued use of Formation Bible Study after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the changes.
