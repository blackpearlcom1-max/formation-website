Legal & Privacy

Cookie Policy

We believe in full transparency. Here's exactly what cookies Formation Bible Study uses, why we use them, and how you're always in control.

Effective: April 16, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Formation Bible Study is a Bible study app built on trust. This policy explains our cookie practices in plain language — no legal jargon. Questions? Email us at privacy@formation.app and we'll be glad to help.

Section 01

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web-based app. They help services remember information about you — such as your login status, preferences, or which pages you've visited — so your experience is smoother and more personalized on each return visit.

Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses. They simply store small pieces of data that help services work better for you. When we say "cookies" in this policy, we also include similar technologies like local storage, session storage, and tracking pixels.

Cookies are either first-party (set directly by Formation Bible Study) or third-party (set by external services we use). They may be session cookies that expire when you close your browser, or persistent cookies that stay on your device for a set period.

Section 02

Types of Cookies We Use

Formation Bible Study uses four categories. The first is required for the app to work; the other three are optional and can be managed through your browser.

🔒

Essential Cookies

Required

These make Formation Bible Study work. They handle authentication, keep your session active, and protect against security threats. You cannot opt out — without them, the app cannot function.

📊

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These help us understand how people use Formation Bible Study in aggregate — which features get used, where users drop off, and how the app can be improved. Data is anonymized and never used to identify individuals.

📣

Marketing Cookies

Optional

These allow us to show relevant Formation Bible Study content on other platforms and measure how well our campaigns perform. You can opt out at any time through your browser or the links in Section 5.

⚙️

Preference Cookies

Optional

These remember your choices — preferred Bible translation, display settings, and study preferences — so you don't have to re-enter them each visit.

Section 06

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers transmit "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals indicating a preference not to be tracked. Because there is currently no industry-standard interpretation of these signals, Formation Bible Study does not alter its data practices in response to them. We will update this section if a recognized standard is established.

Section 07

Children's Privacy

Formation Bible Study is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly use cookies to collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe we have done so inadvertently, please contact us at privacy@formation.app and we will promptly delete the information.

Section 08

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our technology, legal obligations, or practices change. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also notify you through the app or by email.

Continued use of Formation Bible Study after any updates constitutes your acknowledgment of the revised policy.
